Welcome to ***Extreme Trivia Tac Toe*****

You must enter a username to play the game.  You can also enter your email address so that others can challenge you to a game.

Choose either Player 1 or Player 2. 
If no other players are online, you can invite a friend, or challenge a previous player. 

Once Player 1 and Player 2 are selected, the game will start and other players must wait until the game is complete in order to start a new game. 

Player 1 will choose the category.
Then player 1 will choose the difficulty level.

When the game starts, the 15 second timer will begin.  
Player 1 must select a square on the 3 x 3 grid. 
Both players will be given the remaining time to answer the question.

If you answer the question first your icon will appear in that square. 
If your opponent answers that question first their icon will appear in that square.
Then the next player selects a square.
If time runs out or if no one anwers the question correctly, the next player gets to select a square.

The first player with 3 squares in a row wins the game!
